Discussion Highlights:

Mr. Ari Sky, Deputy Town Administrator / Finance Director presented this article. This is the standard semi-annual appropriation to fund Natick Pegasus from fees collected from cable subscribers and remitted to the town by the three cable license holders operating in Natick. The committee discussed the need for planning alternative funding sources for Pegasus, since the hardwired cable subscription model is declining as streaming becomes more popular.
