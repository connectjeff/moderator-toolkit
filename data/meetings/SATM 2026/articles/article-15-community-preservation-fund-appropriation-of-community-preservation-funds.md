## Warrant Text

To see if the Town will vote to appropriate the Community Preservation Fund annual revenues into each of the Community Preservation Act (CPA) accounts, which include Community (Affordable) Housing, Open Space and Recreation, and Historic Resources; and further appropriate annual revenues for administration and operation for use by the Community Preservation Committee (CPC) in Fiscal Year 2027, in accordance with General Laws Chapter 44B, or otherwise act thereon.

## Finance Committee

Recommendation: Favorable
Vote: 11-0-0
Unanimous: unanimously favorable
Date voted: March 12, 2026
Motion vote threshold: Requires a Majority Vote

Discussion Highlights:

Community Preservation Committee Chair Mr. Bill Ramrage and CED Director Amanda Loomis presented Article 15 to the Finance Committee. The CPC is not bringing forward any specific projects at the Spring 2026 Town Meeting. The appropriation requested under this article is to fund the CPC’s Administration and Operation fund in the amount of 5% of the 110 revenue collected since the last Town Meeting appropriation. The request further specifies that the spending under that request be earmarked for 50% support of a Community Planner position, not to exceed $40,000. The Finance Committee reviewed the general process of the CPA/CPC, as well as the documentation of funding receipts. The committee vote unanimously to support the Article 15 appropriation requests.
