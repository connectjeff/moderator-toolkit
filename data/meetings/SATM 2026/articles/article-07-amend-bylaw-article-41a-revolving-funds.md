Discussion Highlights:

Jon Marshall, Deputy Administrator of Operations, presented this Article 7, which is a cleanup and rewrite of the existing bylaw on town revolving funds, Bylaw 41A. It does not create any new revolving funds or authorize any expenditures (companion Article 8 will authorize expenditures from the revolving funds). Review of existing Bylaw 41A was prompted by the establishment of the new stormwater revolving fund by fall 2025 town meeting. This rewrite is based on a model bylaw provided by the state Department of Revenue's Division of Municipal Services (DLS). The primary changes are to add a Purpose section, to revise the description of the annual reporting on each revolving fund, and to move the description of the various funds and purposes into a table. In addition, the revision consistently identifies a department or board (rather than a particular officer of a department or board)...
