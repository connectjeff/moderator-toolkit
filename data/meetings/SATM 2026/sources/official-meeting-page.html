2026 Spring Annual Town Meeting

2026 Spring Annual Town Meeting will continue Thursday April 30th at 7:30 PM at the Kennedy Middle School Auditorium located at 165 Mill Street.

For all Town Meeting members, it is crucial for you to arrive on time for the start of this session, such that we can begin business promptly at 7:30pm once a quorum is reached.  

The agenda for Session #2 of SATM 2026 is as follows:

  • Welcome Message and Introduction
  • Newly Elected Town Meeting Members' Oath of Office
  • Pledge of Allegiance and Moment of Silence
  • Procedural Motions
  • Warrant Articles 3, 9, 12, 13, 16, and 17.

Unofficial voting results for SATM 2026

We will be working through the remaining Articles in the order they appear on the Warrant, with changes to Article order having been made through procedural motions or use of a consent agenda. We will take up Articles in this order until the meeting is adjourned or disolved.  Therefore, the set of articles listed in the agenda is intended solely as an estimate of the the business we may cover in this session of Town Meeting.  

All motions and amendments received by the Moderator are posted to this webpage.  Town Meeting members should review this webpage prior to each meeting to familiarize yourself with all motions that may come to the floor of Town Meeting.

Town Meeting Members are reminded and encouraged to sign up for email and/or text alerts related to Town Meeting using the Alerts & Notifications feature of the Town website (see: natickma.gov/list.aspx and select “Town Meeting”).  The agenda for each night of Town Meeting will be through an alert will be sent if you subscribe. These notifications are the only messages that will be sent regarding the agenda and supplemental material in advance of each night.  

With your participation, I look forward to a successful Town Meeting.

Jeff Alderson, Town Moderator
