2025 Fall Annual Town Meeting

FATM 2025 Session #4 on Thursday October 30th, 2025 at KMS

Fall Annual Town Meeting 2025 will continue on Tuesday 10/30/25 at 7:30 PM at the Kennedy Middle School Auditorium located at 165 Mill Street.  

The agenda for Session #4 of FATM 2025 is as follows:


  • Welcome Message and Introduction
  • Newly Elected Town Meeting Members' Oath of Office
  • Pledge of Allegiance
  • Moment of Silence, in honor of John R. Culkin
  • Assembly of Town Meeting
  • Announcements
  • Warrant Articles 25, 26, 30 (only to hear a report of the CBRC), and 31

For Town Meeting members, it is crucial for you to arrive on time for the start of this session, such that we can begin business promptly at 7:30pm once a quorum is reached.  While the remainder of business before Town Meeting is limited, we will not be able to proceed with any business until a quorum is present.  I ask that everyone make their best efforts to attend on time so that we can finish the business before the town this Thursday.


We will be working through the remaining Articles in the order they appear on the Warrant, with changes to Article order having been made through procedural motions at Session #1.  We will take up Articles in this order until the meeting is adjourned.  Therefore, the set of articles listed in the agenda is intended solely as an estimate of the the business we may cover in this session of Town Meeting.  


All motions and amendments received by the Moderator are posted to this webpage.  Town Meeting members should review this webpage prior to each meeting to familiarize yourself with all motions that may come to the floor of Town Meeting.


Town Meeting Members are reminded and encouraged to sign up for email and/or text alerts related to Town Meeting using the Alerts & Notifications feature of the Town website (see: natickma.gov/list.aspx and select “Town Meeting”).  The agenda for each night of Town Meeting will be through an alert will be sent if you subscribe. These notifications are the only messages that will be sent regarding the agenda and supplemental material in advance of each night.  


With your participation, I look forward to a successful Town Meeting.


Jeff Alderson, Town Moderator
