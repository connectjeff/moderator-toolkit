## Warrant Text

To see if the Town will vote to accept Jennifer Circle as a public way, and any appurtenant easements thereto, as laid out by the Select Board and as shown on a plan or plans on file at the Department of Public Works and the Office of the Town Clerk; To see if the Town will vote to authorize the Select Board to acquire by gift, purchase, eminent domain or otherwise, easements in any land necessary for laying out and acceptance of Jennifer Circle, and any appurtenant drainage, utility or other easements related to said Jennifer Circle and/or accept grants thereof; and, further, to authorize the Select Board and other applicable Town of Natick boards and personnel to take all related actions necessary or appropriate to accomplish the purposes of this article; or otherwise act thereon.

Motion vote threshold: Requires a 2/3rds Vote

Discussion Highlights:

Mr. Errickson, Town Administrator, presented this article. He explained that this is a roadway that is seeking to be accepted as a public way. This is a small subdivision that was constructed in the 90s, but it was never accepted at that time as a public way. The Select Board adopted standards for accepting roadways. The residents on Jennifer Circle went through the standard process and the road scored very well on that process. The Select Board has endorsed the advancement of Jennifer Circle through the program. Motions & Debate A member asked why the roads presented this Town Meeting for acceptance are not public roads already. Mr. Errickson said it was different for each Article. Usually what happens is a developer builds a subdivision but then needs to submit the roadway to the town for acceptance but that doesn’t always happen.
