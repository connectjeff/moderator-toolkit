Mr. Townsend presented this article. He shared that the appropriation will be sourced from State Aid in an amount just under $60,000. He shared that we budget our State Aid number based on the Governor’s initial estimates. However, State Aid exceeded the initial estimate by over $200,000, and is the funding source for this Article. He noted that there is a bill for the golf course in this Article, which we don’t usually do for Enterprise funds.
