Discussion Highlights:

Questions and debate by committee members surfaced repeated concerns about the impact of this rezoning on the nearby residential neighborhood in West Natick. The lot coverage limit would increase from its current level of 25% to 30%. The building height limit would increase from 30 feet currently to 2.5 stories and 35 feet. Concern was expressed that the resulting move of the boundary of residential zoning would shift the buffer zones called for around the commercial areas that are subject to agreements between Natick and Framingham, and that the shift would move those buffer zones closer to existing homes in the neighboring portion of Natick. This concern was disputed but not resolved to the satisfaction of some committee members.
