## Moderator Summary

Article 27 asks Town Meeting to act on `Community Preservation Funds: General Appropriation of Community Preservation Funds`, sponsored by Community Preservation Committee. The official warrant text should be reviewed before this summary is finalized.

## Warrant Text

To see if the Town will vote to appropriate from the Community Preservation Fund annual revenues in the amounts identified by the Community Preservation Committee in the amount equal to ten percent for each of the following CPA categories, which include Community (Affordable) Housing, Parks and Open Space, and Historic Preservation; or otherwise act thereon.
