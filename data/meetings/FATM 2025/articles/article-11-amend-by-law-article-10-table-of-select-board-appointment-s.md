Discussion Highlights:

This motion was presented by Select Board member Richard Sidney. Mr. Sidney explained that the Select Board believes that the current table of Select Board appointments is outdated and includes some boards and/or committees that are no longer needed and which the Select Board is not otherwise required (by state law, Town Charter, Town Bylaws or intermunicipal agreement) to make. The motion is not intended to limit the scope of the Select Board’s appointments, but to list only those appointments about which the Select Board has no discretion. The Select Board could then continue to make (or cease to make) discretionary appointments without coming to Town Meeting to amend the table.
