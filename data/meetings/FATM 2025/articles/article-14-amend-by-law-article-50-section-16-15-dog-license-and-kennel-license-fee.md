## Finance Committee

Recommendation: Favorable
Vote: 12-0-0
Unanimous: unanimously favorable
Date voted: 9/11/2025
Motion vote threshold: Requires a Majority Vote

Discussion Highlights:

This motion was presented by Town Clerk, Andrew Ghobrial. Mr Ghobrial explained that this motion would remove the dog license and kennel fees from the Town Bylaws so that the Select Board would be able to adjust such fees from time to time without Town Meeting needing to change to the Bylaws, but that no changes to the fees were being sought at this time. Finance Committee members asked about the required process to change the fees if this motion were to pass. Mr. Ghobrial stated that no hearing would be required, but if the Select Board were to consider changes to the fees, it would do so only in a public meeting, as required by the Open Meeting law. 80 In response to a Finance Committee member, Mr. Ghobrial stated that the fees for unlicensed kennels were intentionally omitted from the scope of this motion as those fees are enforced by the police through the Animal Control Officer....

Draft Arguments For (reviewer-confirm):

- The committee voted 12-0-0 to recommend Favorable Action on the subject matter of Article 14.

Draft Arguments Against (reviewer-confirm):

- Several Finance Committee members indicated their view that this would be helpful in streamlining the current onerous process to revise such fees, but expressed some concern that a streamlined process would carry some risk that less awareness of proposed changes would result.
