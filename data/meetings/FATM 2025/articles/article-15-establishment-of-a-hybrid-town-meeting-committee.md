Article 15 asks Town Meeting to act on `Establishment of a Hybrid Town Meeting Committee`, sponsored by Town Moderator and Select Board . The official warrant text should be reviewed before this summary is finalized.

## Warrant Text

To see what action(s) the Town will take to establish a Hybrid Town Meeting Committee, in accordance with Section 2-11 (e) of the Town of Natick Home Rule Charter, to study and recommend a framework for conducting hybrid town meetings, to determine how the committee shall be constituted , to see what sum of money the Town will vote to raise and appropriate, transfer from available funds, or otherwise provide for the operation of the committee; or otherwise act thereon .

The Town Moderator appeared at the hearing for this article. The Moderator surveyed Town Meeting members following the 2025 Spring Annual Town Meeting. 28% of respondents indicated that they would attend remotely or consider remote attendance. Medical issues were cited by many of those as a motivating reason. Finance Committee members inquired about the timeline and about the composition of the study committee.
