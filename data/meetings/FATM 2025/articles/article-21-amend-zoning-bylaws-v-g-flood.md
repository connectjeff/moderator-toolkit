Discussion Highlights:

This motion was presented by Director of Community and Economic Development, Amanda Loomis. Ms. Loomis explained that the purpose of this motion is to delete a bylaw that is not currently being utilized and which would prohibit construction anywhere in Natick because of how broadly it is written. She noted that the Flood Plain bylaw and the Stormwater Management bylaw are intended to address the same concern. One Finance Committee member noted that zoning bylaws are written stating limits on what property owners can do on their property, but that the reason for such restrictions is to protect abutting property owners.
