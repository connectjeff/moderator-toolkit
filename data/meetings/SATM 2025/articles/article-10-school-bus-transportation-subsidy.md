## Moderator Summary

Article 10 asks Town Meeting to act on `School Bus Transportation Subsidy`, sponsored by Superintendent of Schools. The official warrant text should be reviewed before this summary is finalized.

## Warrant Text

To see if the Town will vote to appropriate and raise, or transfer from available funds, a sum of money for the purpose of operation and administration of the school bus transportation system, and to reduce or offset fees charged for students who elect to use the school bus transportation system for transportation to and from school, for Fiscal Year 2026 (July 1, 2025 through June 30, 2026); or otherwise act thereon.
