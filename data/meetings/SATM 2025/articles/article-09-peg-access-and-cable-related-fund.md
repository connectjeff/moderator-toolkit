## Warrant Text

To see what sum of money the Town will vote to appropriate from the PEG Access and Cable Related Fund, established by vote of 2019 Special Town Meeting #1 under Article 1, as authorized by Chapter 44, Section 53F ¾ of the General Laws, as amended, to fund PEG access programming, as well as certain other municipal cable related expenses; or otherwise act thereon.

## Finance Committee

Recommendation: Favorable
Vote: 14-0-0
Unanimous: unanimously favorable
Date voted: April 1, 2025
Motion vote threshold: Requires a Majority Vote

Discussion Highlights:

This recurring appropriation request transfers funds the Town has collected from the three licensed Cable providers doing business in town to Natick Pegasus - the separate legal entity providing community cable services and access. These funds are ultimately paid by the cable subscribers. It was again noted that the business model for community cable services is changing as the number of cable subscribers decreases - and that a plan needs to be developed to continue services as the current revenue source decreases. The committee voted 14-0-0 to recommend Favorable Action on the subject matter of Article 9.
